Our Values

  • Courage – We make tough choices when needed, and do so in an open and inclusive culture that encourages speaking up.
  • Determination – We know drug discovery is a tough business. Even though 90% of drug candidates fail, we are going to make it!
  • Honesty – We are responsible for our success, and we will make it happen right.
  • Efficiency – We only have so much money, so we better make it count!
